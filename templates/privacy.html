Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you use the Spendly expense tracking service.

By using Spendly, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and session timestamps, collected automatically to improve the Service.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

We do not collect payment card details, bank account numbers, or any sensitive financial credentials.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Spendly Service.
  • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure.
  • Personalise your experience and remember your preferences.
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs.
  • Send important service notifications such as password resets or policy updates.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard measures — including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and hashed password storage — to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Spendly account.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently erased from our systems within 30 days. You may also request deletion of specific records at any time from your account settings.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or in response to a valid legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Spendly, our users, or the public.
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating the Service, under strict confidentiality agreements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data.
  • Portability — export your expense data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — opt out of non-essential data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies strictly necessary for authentication and keeping you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so may prevent you from logging in to the Service.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. For significant changes, we will notify you via an in-app message or email before the changes take effect.

Questions or concerns about your privacy? Contact us at privacy@spendly.app — we aim to respond within 48 hours.
